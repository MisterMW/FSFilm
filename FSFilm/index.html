Welcome to opening day for Five Days in Falun on June 1, what a day it will be

Five Days in Falun - at last it's here! Set in the dark forests of Swedish rural town Falun this deeply passionate drama will keep you on edge all the way through the closing scenes.
Featuring this year's shining star Anna-Lisa Ericsson and top romantic actor Folke Filbyter, director Holger Hatt has managed to create a truly compelling story where the main characters
will battle both heaven and hell in their efforts to keep a large and divided family together.'
